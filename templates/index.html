🚀 The Tech Behind Farmlens AI

At Farmlens AI, we aim to simplify advanced agricultural technology for everyday farmers. Our current system uses K-Means clustering to segment crop images and identify healthy and unhealthy regions.

The analysis is based on foundational image processing techniques and logical evaluation. While the current model provides useful insights, it is built with simplicity and accessibility in mind.

Our goal is not just accuracy, but adoption — helping farmers become comfortable with smart farming technologies and AI-driven decision making.

🚧 We are actively developing more advanced models including deep learning, disease detection, and real-time monitoring systems to enhance precision and reliability.

🌱 Farmlens is a step towards a smarter, more connected agricultural future.
